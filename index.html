The Flow

From submission to bind — in twelve minutes.

What used to require four handoffs, two inspections, and a week of back-and-forth now runs as a single continuous pipeline. Underwriters review exceptions; Caliper handles the rest.

STEP 01

Intake

Submission arrives via agent portal, API, or carrier CRM. Caliper pulls public-record data, peril maps, and previous loss history within the same minute.

0 — 90 sec
STEP 02

Photo Review

Multimodal models segment every image, score 56 condition factors, and reconcile against tax-assessor records. Defects are flagged with bounding-box citations.

90 sec — 3 min
STEP 03

Voice Verification

If gaps remain, Caliper places an outbound call. Homeowner answers confirm or clarify — Mandarin, Spanish, ASL relay, whatever they speak.

3 — 9 min
STEP 04

Bind or Refer

A signed underwriting memo with citations lands in the queue. Clear-cut files auto-bind. Exceptions route to a human with the full evidence package attached.

9 — 12 min
